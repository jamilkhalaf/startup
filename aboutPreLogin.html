About the Pacman Game

Welcome to the Pacman game, a modern twist on the nostalgic arcade classic! Our platform combines the beloved gameplay of Pacman with modern web technologies to create an engaging experience. Players navigate a maze, collect points by eating pellets, and avoid ghosts.

Key features include:

  • Real-time leaderboard updates to track your high scores.
  • A responsive design for both desktop and mobile gameplay.
  • Random fun images from the Dog API after every game to celebrate your progress!

Log in or sign up to start playing and see how high you can climb on the leaderboard. Challenge yourself, have fun, and enjoy the blend of nostalgia and technology!
